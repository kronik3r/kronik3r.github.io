unevenly sustained attention

Exploring ideas about programming
Thinking of the movie where’s my friend’s house? Makes me think of Noor and the innocence of childhood and the importance of primary emotions and virtues. Noor seems to be curious and welcoming and playful. This makes me think about my own primary virtues, the once I lost and the ones I no longer believe in. I hope that having her and being around her will teach me again how to find those emotions.

Coming back to the movie for a second, it really makes me feel like I am witnessing someone bringing a diamond out of some muddy and violent river. Not only was the director able to extract it out of this impossible place but, also able to hold it in the clear of daylight, for us the audience and turn it left and right for our close examination. And we’re just mesmerized by how pure and real it is and in turn makes us go look for our own diamonds in our own muddy rivers and for those who have some cheap knock off version of it, to throw it away and go looking for the real thing.
